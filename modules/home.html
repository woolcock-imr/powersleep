Help us learn more about sleep and cognitive performance
Do you sleep well?
If so, we invite you to be involved in research to help improve our understanding of enhancing slow wave sleep on cognitive performance.
The first step is for you to register your interest. 

You will then be sent a personal email with a link to our questionnaire asking about your sleep,general health and lifestyle. 

Eligible participants are invited to the Woolcock Institute, Glebe to undertake 6 overnight studies (3 consecutive nights on two occasions one week apart). You will be required to sleep in the day and stay awake for measurements of cognition and performance throughout the night.
